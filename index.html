Brief Description: A mind control program run by the CIA from the 1950’s to the early 60’s. The purpose of the program was to discover methods of reconstructing human minds, replacing current and implanting new ones. The newly reconstructed minds would be made within the CIA’s best interest, such as gaining intelligence from the enemy.
